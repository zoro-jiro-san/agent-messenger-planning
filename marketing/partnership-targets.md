## Outreach Sequence (per target)

1. **Warm intro request** (via LinkedIn/Twitter mutuals) — preferred
2. **Cold email** (150 words, specific to their stack) — fallback
3. **Waitlist invite + demo access link** — if email engages
4. **Discovery call (15 min)** — align use case
5. **Pilot agreement (4 weeks, free)** — simple MOU
6. **Go-live + joint announcement** — within 6 weeks of agreement

---

## Pilot Agreement Checklist

- [ ] Pilot scope defined (which features, expected msgs/users)
- [ ] Success criteria documented (e.g., "20 exchanges/day with <1s latency")
- [ ] Timeline (start → review → go/no-go decision)
- [ ] Data/privacy terms (GDPR compliance statement)
- [ ] Referral/Testimonial release (optional)
