## Emergency Contacts (When Things Go Wrong)

**Problem**: Low initial velocity (<30 stars first day)  
**Action**: Mobilize Twitter network, post in additional subreddits, email waitlist with urgency ("launching now" subject)

**Problem**: Product Hunt ranking stuck outside Top 10  
**Action**: Post 3 updates with new screenshots/user testimonials; engage every comment; tweet PH link with #ProductHunt hashtag

**Problem**: GitHub issues piling up, no time to respond  
**Action**: Draft canned responses for common issues (installation, auth, integration); recruit 1–2 community moderators from power users

**Problem**: No tutorial content produced by week 2  
**Action**: Repurpose README quickstart as Dev.to post (better than nothing); record short Loom walkthrough yourself
